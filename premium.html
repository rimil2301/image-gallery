Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For monthly plans, you'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Yearly plans are refundable within 30 days.

What's the difference between free and premium?

Free members get access to basic collections with standard resolution images. Premium unlocks all content, 4K downloads, advanced search, and exclusive collections.

How many devices can I use with my account?

Your premium account can be used on up to 5 devices simultaneously. You can access your account from any web browser or our mobile apps.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes, we offer 40% discounts for students and educators. Please contact our support team with proof of your academic status to get a discount code.

Can I use these images commercially?

Yes, all premium images come with a commercial license allowing use in websites, blogs, advertisements, and other commercial projects. Some restrictions apply for resale.
